Save even more manual effort when creating and publishing

The BrandGhost team has been busy!

We’re excited to roll out three major updates designed to make your cross-platform content scheduling smoother than ever — and give creators more ways to grow their audience with less effort.

Post Directly to Telegram

You asked, we delivered.

BrandGhost now supports direct posting to Telegram channels and groups.

Whether you use Telegram for community updates, paid membership content, or just a loyal fan base, you can now schedule posts, share media, and automate your communication from inside BrandGhost, just like you do with X, Threads, LinkedIn, and other supported platforms.

Why it matters:

  • Keep your Telegram and social channels in sync with your social media posts

  • Eliminate manual posting to telegram

Import Your LinkedIn Posts into Topic Streams

If you’ve ever written a great LinkedIn post that deserved more visibility: this one’s for you.

You can now import your existing LinkedIn content into BrandGhost to automatically populate your Topic Streams. That means your proven, high-performing posts can be reused, remixed, or scheduled to other networks without starting from scratch.

Perfect for:

  • Turning top-performing posts into evergreen content.

  • Maintaining a consistent brand voice across platforms.

  • Saving time by letting BrandGhost recycle what already works.

Your past content is now your future content engine.

Smart Auto-Sizing for Media

Social platforms can be picky about image ratios and video dimensions. That’s why we built Smart Auto-Sizing, a new feature that automatically adjusts your media to fit the ideal format for each network.

Whether it’s a tall LinkedIn image, a square Instagram grid post, or a widescreen YouTube thumbnail, BrandGhost ensures it looks right every time.

Benefits:

  • Save hours resizing visuals manually.

  • Consistent, professional-looking posts across all networks.

  • Post once — look perfect everywhere.

One Platform, Every Channel

With these new updates, BrandGhost continues to make it easier for creators and marketers to grow across multiple platforms, without the chaos of managing dozens of tools.

✅ Telegram Posting ✅ LinkedIn Imports for Topic Streams ✅ Smart Auto-Sizing for Media

Frequently Asked Questions

Does BrandGhost support posting to Telegram?

Yes, BrandGhost supports direct posting to Telegram channels and groups, letting you schedule and automate your Telegram content alongside all other platforms from one dashboard.

How do I import my LinkedIn posts into BrandGhost?

BrandGhost's LinkedIn import feature lets you pull existing LinkedIn posts directly into Topic Streams so your proven content can be reused, remixed, or scheduled to other networks.

What is Smart Auto-Sizing in BrandGhost?

Smart Auto-Sizing automatically adjusts your images and videos to the ideal dimensions for each social network, ensuring your media looks professional on LinkedIn, Instagram, YouTube, and more.

Can I manage all my social platforms from one place in BrandGhost?

Yes, BrandGhost provides a single dashboard where you can create, schedule, and publish content to Telegram, LinkedIn, X, Instagram, Threads, and other supported platforms simultaneously.
